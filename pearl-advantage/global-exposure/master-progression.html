Master's Progression In the UK and the USA

Applicants meeting eligibility criteria, including portfolio review, can pursue Master’s degrees at the UK and the USA partner universities solely with the Pearl Academy, without requiring an Indian degree or additional qualifications. The applications will be direct to the partner university.

International Partner Universities

Nottingham Trent University, UK

Nottingham Trent University (NTU) is recognised as one of the UK’s truly outstanding universities, with a commitment to student satisfaction, graduate employment, and teaching quality.  Named as The Times and Sunday Times Modern University of the Year in 2023, and the WhatUni University of the Year 2023 (as voted for by students), NTU has been named the University of the Year four times between 2018-2023.  Best known for its Art, Design, Fashion and Creative Arts programmes, it is ranked #=595 in the QS World University Rankings in 2024, and #51-100 in subject rankings for Art & Design.  Located right in the heart of one of the UK’s most exciting cities, NTU is surrounded by – and contributes to – an originality, energy and imagination that makes Nottingham a very special place to live, work and create.

University of Creative Arts, UK

For 160 years, the University for the Creative Arts has championed creativity, fostering vibrant, innovative, and human global communities. Tailoring education to individual paths, UCA stands as the UK's premier creative speciality university. Offering courses in Architecture, Crafts, Fashion, Graphic Design, Illustration, Fine Art, Photography, Film, Media, Performing Arts, and Creative Business, UCA's campuses in Surrey, Kent, and abroad serve as hubs for forging partnerships, collaborating with experts, and addressing real-world challenges. UCA's excellence is underscored by The Guardian ranking it second among specialist creative universities in the UK for 2023, while The Times/Sunday Times Good University Guide 2023 positions UCA as the third specialist creative university. Additionally, UCA's School of Fashion & Textiles secures a noteworthy 12th rank in the UK, according to The Guardian's 2023 assessment.

Academy of Arts University, USA

Established in 1929 in San Francisco, the Academy of Art University stands as an esteemed and accredited institution, holding the distinction of being the largest private art and design school in the US. Renowned for its exceptional curriculum tailored for art and design proficiency, it has nurtured numerous accomplished professionals in diverse fields. With a current enrollment of over 7,900 art students in 2022 and a legacy spanning more than 90 years, it has fostered a thriving artistic community. The Academy of Art University holds the 94th position among Regional Universities West, standing at the 84th position for quality and value among its peers.

LIM College USA

Established in 1939 as the Laboratory Institute of Merchandising, LIM College has cultivated expertise in the realm of fashion and lifestyle business through a hands-on, experiential approach. Its mission centres on empowering students to realise their career aspirations. Situated in the heart of New York City, LIM College provides unparalleled access to an array of professional prospects and industry connections. While LIM had previously been acknowledged for its excellence in graduate fashion business education, it wasn't until 2019 that the College's undergraduate programs became eligible for recognition. This year, BoF introduced "Badges of Excellence," highlighting institutions' strengths in key areas instead of numerical rankings. LIM College is acclaimed as one of The Best Fashion Schools in the World 2019 in BoF's comprehensive assessment of fashion education.

The University of Law, UK

As one of the UK's longest-standing specialised institutions for legal education and business training, the University of Law (ULaw) maintains campuses across London, Birmingham, Bristol, Guildford, Leeds, Manchester, and Nottingham, as well as international campuses in Berlin and Hong Kong. ULaw's programs are available in online, full-time, and part-time formats, encompassing Professional Development and undergraduate levels. A distinguished faculty of over 250 tutors, comprising qualified lawyers and seasoned business professionals, imparts their extensive practical expertise to our students. Anchored in a well-established teaching approach, ULaw emphasises the acquisition of knowledge and practical skills, effectively equipping students to tackle real-world challenges and embark on successful career paths.

Arts University Bournemouth, UK

Founded in 1880 as the Bournemouth Government School of Art, Arts University Bournemouth has evolved over time to encompass science, design, and technology. In 2012, it achieved full university status, specialising in art, performance, design, and media within the creative industries. The university offers a comprehensive range of programs at the graduate, postgraduate, and research levels, including a variety of short courses. With approximately 20 undergraduate and 10 graduate courses, the institution is divided into two faculties: Art and Design, and Media and Performance. Distinguished by its specialised focus, Arts University Bournemouth stands as one of the few institutions in the UK devoted to fostering a creative environment. Here, faculty and students share a profound enthusiasm for Arts and Design. The University for the Creative Arts is positioned at #71 in The Complete University Guide List 2023. According to the Guardian University Rankings 2023, it claims the 70th spot in the rankings.
