International Bachelor's Degree In the UK & Australia

Pearl Academy extends the Bachelor's Progression option to all the undergraduate students, providing them with a chance to pursue an international degree through our select partner academic universities. This opportunity is available to UG students after completing the Advanced Diploma level with Pearl Academy. Upon successful completion of the program at the partner university, students will be awarded a bachelor's degree from the respective partner university. Pearl Academy facilitates students in the application process, subject to students meeting the entry & eligibility conditions of the partner universities.

International Partner Universities

More than 240 applicants have chosen to seize this valuable opportunity since 2019.

Nottingham Trent University, UK

Nottingham Trent University (NTU) is recognized as one of the UK’s truly outstanding universities, with a commitment to student satisfaction, graduate employment, and teaching quality. Named University of the Year three times in the last few years including Guardian University of the year, 2019, Times and Sunday Times Modern University of the Year 2018 and Times Higher Education University of the Year 2017. Offers over 500 full-time courses and they all offer opportunities to get practical experience in your subject.

Nottingham Trent University is best known for its Art and Design, Fashion, and creative arts programmes, but it also hosts one of the UK’s largest law schools. NTU is ranked 42nd in the Complete University Guide 2024 and in the Guardian University Guide 2024. Rated GOLD in Teaching Excellence Framework (TEF). NTU is the 4th most sustainable university in the world.

The institute's lecturers have extensive experience in industry, research, and professions, which is both nationally and internationally recognized. You’ll learn so much from them about the theory and the practice of your subject. Whatever course you do you’ll find new, exciting ideas and develop the confidence to form your own opinions and build up practical experience in the subject.

NTU will provide the highest possible standards of teaching and research and make the best possible facilities available to you during your studies. Nottingham is a great city to be a student.

University for Creative Arts, UK

Creativity makes global communities more vibrant, more innovative, more human. Which is why, for 160 years, the University for the Creative Arts has been a passionate advocate for creativity, tailoring each student’s education to help everyone to forge the path that’s right for them. UCA is the UK’s highest-ranked creative specialist university, offering courses in Architecture, Crafts, Fashion, Graphic Design, Illustration, Fine Art, Photography, Film, Media, the Performing Arts and Creative Business. But that’s not the whole story. The University’s campuses across Surrey and Kent, and overseas, are places to forge inspiring partnerships, work with creative experts and build real solutions to human problems. The Guardian has ranked UCA the second specialist creative university in the UK for 2023. The Times/Sunday Times Good University Guide 2023 has ranked UCA the third placed specialist creative university in the UK. UCA's School of Fashion & Textiles is ranked 12th in the UK by The Guardian for 2023.

Manchester Metropolitan University, UK

Manchester Metropolitan University is home to a diverse international student population from more than 130 countries. The University is ranked amongst the world’s top 200 universities under 50 years old (Times Higher Education Young University Rankings 2020), which is a measure of its commitment to delivering and supporting quality research and teaching. Manchester Metropolitan’s campus is located at the center of Manchester, amongst world-renowned music, arts, shopping, and sports venues and the second largest Chinatown in Britain.

Manchester Metropolitan University ranks #67 as per The Complete University Guide - University Ranking 2023. In The Guardian Ranking 2023, Manchester Met is ranked #15 in Fashion & Textiles, #3 in Animation & Game Design, #9 in Graphic Design, #11 in Interior Design subject. Rated Gold award in the Teaching Excellence Framework (TEF 2024)

The courses at Manchester Fashion Institute cover all areas of the fashion industry, from design to business management, buying and promotion. The Institute works with industry partners to ensure its courses combine academic expertise with industry experience – making sure its graduates are ready for a career in the global fashion industry.

Pearl Academy students have an option to obtain an international bachelor’s degree by pursuing courses at Manchester Metropolitan University in England.

Torrens University, Australia

Torrens University is Australia’s international university, committed to providing relevant and transformational education and producing research which answers society’s pressing questions. As Australia’s first new university in more than 20 years, Torrens University brings a careers-focused and global perspective to Australian higher education. Home to over 19,000 students from 115 different countries, Torrens University has quickly become Australia’s fastest growing University and boasts a credible industry-immersive difference, with 91% of its students beginning their career within 12 months of graduation.

Torrens University has an unwavering commitment to producing global graduates with the skills to form strong industry connections, rooted in a culture of change-making and positive impact on society and environment.

The University of Derby, UK

In the heart of England - the birthplace of the Industrial Revolution - University of Derby is proud to be building on its region’s heritage of innovation with industry - relevant expert teaching and world-leading research. The institute offers higher education in a wide choice of subject areas at the campus in Derby.

The University of Derby has been placed in the Top 50 in The Guardian University Guide 2023, a climb of 11 places. Derby is now ranked 48th out of 121 institutions, moving up from 59th. Pearl Academy students have an option to obtain an internationally recognized bachelor’s degree by pursuing courses at University of Derby in England.

Top 10 for Postgraduate Student Experience 2020, its academic strength is backed up by practical experience to ensure that the students are ready for a successful career, wherever their talents lie. With more than £200 million invested in the last ten years, the institute offers some of the best university facilities in the UK. As Derbyshire’s only University it uses its academic expertise, research, and innovation to foster talent and promote links with businesses regionally, nationally, and globally.
