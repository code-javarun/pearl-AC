Overview

If you're passionate about fashion, media, and communication, and you dream of a career where your camera presence and conversational skills help you make a mark in the vibrant, fast-paced world of fashion, this program is for you. The B. Des. in Fashion Communication is an undergraduate course that equips you to create new visual cultures, communicate with ease across mediums, and play a part in rewriting fashion's global language. 

Fashion communication is a dynamic global industry that helps shape a brand’s identity through social media, visual narratives and innovative campaigns. 

Our B. Des. in Fashion Communication program enables our students to gain exposure to diverse fashion markets. We partner with top fashion institutions and industry leaders to offer international exchange opportunities, collaborative projects, internships, and unique immersive experiences.

Program Curriculum

The B. Des. in Fashion Communication program at Pearl Academy offers a transformative learning experience, propelling you into the future of the ever-evolving world of fashion, luxury, and lifestyle.

While studying in India’s sole interdisciplinary multimedia fashion communication program, students get a chance to delve into social media, artificial intelligence, photography, fashion films, event design, fashion shows, creative direction, advertising, PR, fashion journalism, podcasting, trend-hunting, visual research, and every captivating facet of the fashion industry.

The B. Des. in Fashion Communication is a four-year bachelor’s degree program. The course is designed to equip students with the skills, knowledge, infrastructure and experience needed to excel in the fashion communication industry under the mentorship of industry practitioners and well-qualified faculty members. It covers a wide range of subjects to ensure that students develop into well-rounded creative professionals ready to take on real world projects and challenging job roles upon graduation.

The mode of learning

The B. Des. in Fashion Communication program is a comprehensive full-time program, emphasising in-person learning as its core learning methodology. The curriculum is thoughtfully designed to foster hands-on experience and meaningful interactions within the fashion communication field. Students may get online / hybrid schedules for collaborative assignments, industry projects and masterclasses.

The Foundation :

  • Lays down a strong foundation for a career in creative fields, irrespective of educational background.
  • Builds creative confidence of the students by imparting skills, tools and knowledge of the fundamentals in the creative field.
  • Overcomes social & cultural stereotypes about a career in creative fields.
  • Enables problem-solving attitude and multidisciplinary approach to design.
  • Promotes Exploration, Innovation, and Inventiveness.

 

The Foundation curriculum consists of the following:

  • Visual Representation: Covering manual and digital representation ( 2 semesters)
  • Material Explorations ( 2 semesters)
  • Fundamentals of Design ( 2 semester)
  • Digital Tools: Software for visual representation
  • History of Design ( 1 semester)
  • Introduction to specialization ( 1 semester)

Our commitment to practical learning and ensures our students to become fully prepared for the highly competitive industry. Through live industry projects and mandatory summer internships supported by the academic linkages, students have the chance to work on real-world campaigns, fashion shows, and collaborations with leading brands. These hands-on experiences provide a bridge between classroom knowledge and the dynamic fashion communication industry, preparing students to tackle real challenges from day one. 
Our alumni are our pride and testament to the success of our program. Many of our graduates have risen to prominent positions as Fashion Editors, Fashion Filmmakers, and Creative Directors in esteemed fashion houses and media outlets worldwide. Their journeys serve as an inspiration and a reflection of the program's impact on the industry.

Our B. Des. in Fashion Communication program is a comprehensive four-year journey. It begins with a solid foundation in observation, visualization, and fashion history. The first year focuses on essential design skills. From the second year, students explore digital media, visual culture, AR/VR in fashion, and practical fashion shoots. 
In the later stages, they delve into specialized areas like fashion forecasting, trends, fashion filmmaking, and more. They gain exposure to commercial fashion communication, event design, and international projects. The program concludes with a Design Project, preparing graduates for the ever-evolving world of fashion communication. 
Our graduates are well-prepared for diverse roles in the fashion industry and can effectively communicate across various mediums. Join the exciting world of fashion communication to influence fashion's language with creativity and innovation.

We offer a two-week overseas visit to a partner institution at no extra cost. This exposure expands your horizons and gives you a global perspective on fashion communication. 

A mandatory industry internship provides you with real-world experience and valuable industry connections.

A.   Fashion Communication is a specialised domain of fashion studies that focuses on promoting and communicating information about different aspects of fashion. This includes products, services and deliverables using different technologies and media. It involves acquiring knowledge about the fashion and lifestyle industries, and delivering key information via verbal, written, visual and audio-visual means. 

Fashion Communication is a powerful skill that can enable a business to communicate their vision, product offerings and unique positioning to their customers. Courses in this interdisciplinary field are becoming highly sought after in the fashion and lifestyle job markets, as they prepare graduates to manage a brand’s identity and drive direct business impact. 

A.  The scope of B. Des. in Fashion Communication programs in India is rapidly expanding, thanks to the advent of numerous fashion-media houses and increasing public interest in fashion. These programs are designed to equip students with versatile skills that are in high demand across various sectors of the fashion industry.

A B. Des. in Fashion Communication program not only prepares students for traditional roles in public relations (PR) at fashion brands, fashion journalism, and fashion marketing but also opens doors to a multitude of other career paths. Graduates can explore opportunities in visual merchandising, retail space design, styling, photography, events coordination, and fashion advertising.

Moreover, a degree in Fashion Communication paves the way for independent career pursuits such as branding for fashion labels, graphic design, visual arts, freelance fashion journalism, and image consultancy. 

The fashion industry offers countless opportunities, allowing each individual to pick a field most aligned with their passions and aspirations.

A.  Pearl Academy offers a B. Des. in Fashion Communication program with the following eligibility criteria: 

  • Candidates must have passed their 10+2 examination from any recognized board or university.
  • Candidates must have scored a minimum of 50% marks in their 10+2 examination.
  • Candidates appearing in their final examination can also apply provisionally.
  • Besides these above criteria, candidates must clear the Pearl Academy Design Aptitude Test (DAT) and General Proficiency Test (GPT).

The DAT evaluates a candidate's aptitude for creativity, drawing, designability, power of observation and imagination, sensitivity to environment, and communication skills. 

The GPT evaluates a candidate's proficiency in the English language, Logical reasoning, Quantitative Ability & Data Interpretation. 

This is followed by a personal Interview conducted by Pearl Academy for admission into the B. Des. in Fashion Communication program.
