Our Financial Assistance Options

No Cost EMI - For Short-Term Certificate Courses

Pearl Academy has collaborated with GrayQuest, a loan provider financial institution that will help you pay the fees for Short-Term Certificate Courses in equated monthly installments via a 6 months No Cost EMI plan. It is an easy and convenient way to pay your course fee.

Instructions

  • Semester 1 & Onetime fee shall be paid via Demand Draft issued in favour of "CREATIVE ARTS EDUCATION SOCIETY" Or online at https://bit.ly/PAAdmissions
  • Registration & Alumni fees are paid once at the time of admission.
  • Application fee charges once paid are non-refundable.
  • Refunds from the course shall be made as per the Guideline on Refund of Fees to Students available on the website:  
    - Fee Undertaking & Refund Policy for Bachelor's and Master's Programs- Pearl 
    - Fee Undertaking & Refund Policy for Short-Term Certificate Courses- Pearl
  • The security deposit will be refunded after the completion of the course.
  • The fee will increase annually for subsequent years at the discretion of Pearl Academy.
