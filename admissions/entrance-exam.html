Examination Process & Guidelines
Admit cards will be published on 6th June 2024. Examination time & personal Interview details will be mentioned in the Admit card only.

Candidates are required to go through the Demo exam link before the live exam to get the real experience of the Exam. The demo link will be accessible after 24 hours of the application form submission.

Exam Guidelines and FAQs

Exam Pattern

The examination pattern for Pearl Entrance Exam is as follows:

ALL BACHELOR'S & MASTER'S FASHION, INTERIORS, PRODUCT & COMMUNICATION DESIGN PROGRAMS

Student applying for fashion and design courses need to sit for GPT + DAT exam

Exams

GPT + DAT

General Proficiency Test (GPT) + Design Aptitude Test (DAT) | Personal Interview & Portfolio Showcase

Duration: 90 mins of Exam + 30 mins of Personal Interview
