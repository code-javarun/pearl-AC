Regional Campus Director

SHEHNAZ SHAAHID ANKOLKAR

Shehnaz joined Pearl Academy, Mumbai as Campus Manager in April 2022. Since then, she has contributed to the growth and development of the campus in several areas. She worked tirelessly on improving student experience, organizing events and soon became an indispensable part of the Pearl community. She has three decades of experience in various segments including luxury, fashion, lifestyle and education segment and has held multiple roles in capacity of Business Manager, Costume Head and Administrative Head. She is also a great home chef specializing in Bohri cuisine. She hails from Mumbai and has pursued her career in English Literature and has a Post graduate Diploma in Travel and Tourism.
