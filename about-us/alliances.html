Fashion Incubator India

We have partnered with Fashion Incubator India...

Read more

We have partnered with Fashion Incubator India to offer our students curated trips to international fashion weeks across the globe. Students get hands-on experience of working backstage and interning with renowned fashion stalwarts in Paris Fashion Week, Paris Couture Week, New York Fashion Week, London Fashion Week and Dubai Fashion Week. 
Pearl Academy has an exclusive partnership to New York Fashion Week during the most popular season of the year, September. Around 40+ plus Pearl only students along with a faculty travel to NYFW. We also have a 10-days curated program in collaboration with Fashion Incubator India which includes fashion week experience, workshops and masterclasses with industry leaders in digital marketing, fashion photography, styling, media and PR and much more. This 10 days program is a part of students International Lab module.
