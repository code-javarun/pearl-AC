Academic Year 2024 Intake

India's leading institute in Design, Fashion, Business, Filming, Gaming, and Interior Design is proud to announce that the students at Pearl Academy campuses to get UG/PG Degrees effective 2024 intake as enabled by CAES - Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (RGNIYD) by the Act of Parliament No. 35/2012 under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, Government of India. The RGNIYD was set up in 1993 under the Societies Registration Act, XXVII of 1975.

The RGNIYD is an Institution of National Importance established by the Government of India in 1993. It is a premier institution for youth development, research, and training. The RGNIYD offers a wide range of academic programs, including undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in various disciplines related to youth development.

Through this landmark opportunity, Pearl Academy students will also benefit from the RGNIYD's strong focus on research and innovation. We can now anticipate a significant development for the creative industries in India, leading to a new generation of highly skilled and innovative professionals who can propel the sector forward.

Accreditation Timeline -Pearl Academy

Pearl Academy offers 3-year and 4-year Bachelor's programs. Students enrolled in these programs are eligible to receive a degree after their successful completion.

However, if students wish to pursue an Indian or international degree program, Pearl Academy facilitates admission into the same, details of which are as follows:

International Bachelor's Degree

On completion of the Advanced Degree, Pearl Academy students have an option to pursue a Bachelor's degree program from some of the renowned international universities with which Pearl Academy has academic collaborations. These are Nottingham Trent University, University of Derby, Manchester Metropolitan University, University of Creative Arts and Torrens University Australia. Pearl Academy facilitates the enrolment of eligible students in the final year of the Bachelor's degree programs at these universities based on a recognition of and transfer of credits. Students graduate with a degree from the International University.

However, students should note that an international degree is presently not equivalent to an Indian degree as per Association of Indian Universities (AIU) and hence, they will not be able to pursue a master's degree in India unless they have completed the distance education degree facilitated by Pearl.

Students with international degrees are accepted by private and international organizations /MNCs for employment.

Students studying at Pearl Academy campuses will be offered Rajeev Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (RGNIYD) degrees through Creative Arts Education Society (CAES), an implementation partner of RGNIYD. Pearl Academy is a unit of CAES.

International /Industry Exposure

All students pursuing Bachelor's and master's programs at Pearl Academy receive multiple opportunities to gain international industry exposure through international student exchange programs, master classes by global experts, familiarization trips to manufacturing units, export houses, fashion houses and access to international fashion weeks etc.

Accreditation Timeline - Pearl Academy

Pearl Academy offers 3-year and 4-year Undergraduate programs. Students enrolled in these programs are eligible to receive a certificate after the successful completion of each year.

However, if students wish to pursue an Indian or international degree program, Pearl Academy facilitates admission into the same, details of which are as follows:

International Undergraduate Degree

On completion of the Advanced Diploma, Pearl Academy students have an option to pursue a Undergraduate degree program from some of the renowned international universities with which Pearl Academy has academic collaborations. These are Nottingham Trent University, University of Derby, Manchester Metropolitan University, University of Creative Arts and Torrens University Australia. Pearl Academy facilitates the enrolment of eligible students in the final year of the Undergraduate degree programs at these universities based on a recognition of and transfer of credits. Students graduate with a degree from the International University.

However, students should note that an international degree is presently not equivalent to an Indian diploma as per Association of Indian Universities (AIU) and hence, they will not be able to pursue a Postgraduate diploma in India unless they have also completed the distance education degree facilitated by Pearl.

Students with international degrees are accepted by private and international organizations /MNCs for employment.

Indian Undergraduate Degree

Pearl Academy facilitates the enrolment of an Indian undergraduate program so that its students have the option to earn a UGC recognized degree.

Delhi Campus - School of Open Learning, Delhi University.

Mumbai Campus -Institute of Distance and Open Learning, Mumbai. University.

Bangalore Campus - Directorate of Correspondence Courses, Bangalore University. Eligible students from 2022 batch and onwards can get a degree from Bengaluru City University.

Jaipur Campus - Eligible students from 2020 batch and onwards can enrol for BVoc/B.Des degree from Rajasthan ILD Skills University (RISU). Pearl Academy Jaipur Campus is an approved affiliated institute of RISU, a pioneering government skill university recognized by UGC.

Students pursuing Management Programs will be facilitated to enrol for BBA program from IGNOU.

If a student wants to pursue a degree program through ODL (Open and Distance Learning) from a recognized university other than the ones mentioned above, he/she is free to do so.

What Pearl Academy Offers -

4-year Bachelor's program -

End of first year- Foundation Diploma

End of second year- Basic Diploma

End of third year- Advanced Diploma

End of fourth year- Professional Diploma

3-year Bachelor's program -

End of first year- Basic Diploma

End of second year- Advanced Diploma

End of third year- Professional Diploma

Pearl Academy functions as an autonomous institution and does not confer degrees/diplomas approved by UGC /AICTE or any other regulatory authority.

The students will be awarded a postgraduate diploma from Pearl Academy on successful completion. Since Pearl Academy is an autonomous institution, the postgraduate diploma is not accredited by -UGC /AICTE.

International /Industry Exposure

All students pursuing undergraduate and postgraduate diploma programs at Pearl Academy receive multiple opportunities to gain international industry exposure through international student exchange programs, master classes by global experts, familiarization trips to manufacturing units, export houses, fashion houses and access to international fashion weeks etc.
