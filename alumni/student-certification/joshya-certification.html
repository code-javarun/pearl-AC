Certification

We are excited to offer a comprehensive certification in Front-End Development, designed to be completed over a span of 65 days. This program provides participants with in-depth knowledge and practical skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks. Our experienced instructors guide students through hands-on projects and real-world scenarios, ensuring they are well-prepared for industry demands. Upon completion, participants receive a certification that enhances their professional credibility and equips them to build responsive, user-friendly websites and applications. We are confident this certification will open doors to new career opportunities and provide a solid foundation for professional growth.

Course Overview

Our intensive two-month Front-End Development course equips participants with essential skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Angular. Led by industry experts, the program features hands-on projects using tools such as Visual Studio Code and Git, ensuring practical proficiency. Participants benefit from internship opportunities to apply their learning in real-world contexts, supported by comprehensive placement assistance including resume building and interview preparation. This holistic approach prepares graduates for successful careers in front-end development.
