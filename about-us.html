With an Impressive 2-years of Heritage, Grapinz Technology and Institution has established itself as a leading Design Institute, guiding students to outstanding achievements in creative disciplines such as VFX, 3D Animation, Digital Marketing, Adobe, Figma, Internships, and Workshops. Situated in Udumalpet, Tirupur District, Tamil Nadu, we cultivate an environment of creative excellence.

Students across all Grapinz Technology and Institution campuses will benefit from state-of-the-art Tech and Design courses, ensuring they are equipped with the latest industry skills.

Our Heritage

With over two years of nurturing creative talent, Grapinz Technology and Institution has garnered global acclaim for its commitment to excellence, promoting entrepreneurship and employability on a worldwide scale. Since our founding in 2022, we have led the way in embracing technological advancements and strategic industry shifts, continually addressing the dynamic needs of the design and tech industries.

At Grapinz Institution, our courses are meticulously designed to provide graduates with the essential skills required to foster innovation, drive growth, and contribute to social development. We have pioneered the Indian market with our engaging blend of live lectures, creating a highly effective learning environment. As we move forward into an increasingly competitive future, Grapinz Technology and Institution remains dedicated to leading the charge for excellence, ensuring our graduates enter the industry with confidence and expertise.

Our Vision

Our vision propels us to become a leading global institute for fashion and design. Grounded in continuous innovation, we maintain exceptional standards, providing enriching experiences for our students, staff, and industry collaborators.

Excellence and Benchmarks
