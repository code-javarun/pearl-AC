With an Impressive 2-years of Heritage, Grapinz Technology and Institution has established itself as a leading Design Institute, guiding students to outstanding achievements in creative disciplines such as VFX, 3D Animation, Digital Marketing, Adobe, Figma, Internships, and Workshops. Situated in Udumalpet, Tirupur District, Tamil Nadu, we cultivate an environment of creative excellence.

Students across all Grapinz Technology and Institution campuses will benefit from state-of-the-art Tech and Design courses, ensuring they are equipped with the latest industry skills.

Our Vision & Mission

Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals through high-quality education in coding and design, equipping them with the skills needed to thrive in the digital world. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. Additionally, through our IT services, we strive to deliver exceptional technology solutions that drive business growth and efficiency.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading educational institution and we aspire to create an inclusive and dynamic learning environment that inspires creativity and innovation. For our IT services, we envision being the trusted partner for businesses worldwide, known for our excellence in delivering cutting-edge technology solutions that empower organizations to achieve their full potential.
